AI-Powered Health Awareness Chatbot

Ask me about diseases, symptoms, and prevention tips!

HealTrack is a compact, user-friendly health-awareness site that embeds an AI chat interface to provide quick, accessible guidance on common health topics.
Purpose: Teach and assist users about diseases, symptoms, prevention, vaccinations, first aid, nutrition, and exercise.
Interface: A conversational chatbot (Dialogflow Messenger) appears as a floating widget for instant interaction.
Language support: English and Hindi toggleable via the language selector; chat widget language updated accordingly.
Quick actions: FAQ buttons trigger topic-specific events so users get fast, relevant responses.
Design: Clean, centered layout with a calming gradient background and accessible typography for readability.
